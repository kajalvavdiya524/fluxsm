Editions

Compare Editions

Flux is available on-premise, in the cloud, and as a hybrid edition

On-Premise

  • Self-hosted and installed on your network
  • Self contained, no internet connection required
  • Available to all devices on your network
  • Perfect for single-location shops

Cloud

  • Hosted online, nothing to install
  • Access from any device with an internet connection
  • No need to be at the shop to log in
  • Share inventory with other shop locations

Hybrid

  • Self-hosted with online fallback
  • Access without internet, syncs when internet is restored
  • Run reports from anywhere
  • Includes the benefits of both editions
