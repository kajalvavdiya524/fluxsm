Features

What We Offer

Flux offers the features that come standard with all point of sale and shop management systems along with some special features that you won't find anywhere else

Sales Management

  • Track progress of work orders using work order statuses
  • Vehicle makes and models list populated from the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration
  • VIN decoding is also available from the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration
  • Track profit and margins within a work order
  • Customize invoices with a logo and store details
  • Comprehensive reporting to get a clear picture on shop performance

Customer Records

  • Search customers by first name, last name, business name, or phone number
  • Search vehicles by license plate
  • Supports business and personal customer types
  • List multiple contact people per customer regardless of customer type
  • Review customer and vehicle history, available on several pages for easy access when you need it
  • Supports fleet management

Inventory

  • Track inventory for stocked parts & tires
  • Reserve inventory when added to a work order
  • Keep tabs on outside purchase parts & tires
  • Share inventory counts with other locations
  • Detailed reports on cost spent on inventory

Invoicing

  • Work order margin tracking with color-coded buttons
  • Flexible pricing options and discounts on a per-customer basis
  • Generate statements of unpaid invoices per customer
  • Custom payment methods to be able to track exactly how customers pay
  • Allow for split payment on 2 or more payment methods
  • Track invoices paid on credit terms and when account payments are made

Multi-Location

  • Detailed reporting to compare how each location is performing
  • View inventory across all locations
  • Find customers and work history at the store level
  • Set different settings per location, including labor and tax rate
  • Share employee list across all locations, but limit usage to specific locations

Localization

  • Settings can be customized to you, including labor rate, tax rate, and payment methods
  • Timezone is configurable to run in your timezone
  • Customize the details and logo on the printed work orders and invoices
  • Import existing customer data and vehicle history for a seamless switch to Flux

About Us

Flux Shop Manager is a POS and inventory management system for automotive repair and tire shops.
